Section 3.1: Concurrency

2.4 Concurrency

Definition:

In programming, concurrency is the ability of two or more call stacks to be run out of order (asynchronously).

Types of concurrency:

Concurrency is an umbrella that covers exactly two types of concurrent execution:

  1. parallel execution
  2. time-sliced execution
